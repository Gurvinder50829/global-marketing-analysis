🌍 Global Marketing Analysis Dashboard

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of global marketing data to uncover valuable business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends. Using Python-based data analytics and interactive visualizations, the dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven marketing decisions.

The analysis includes customer segmentation, Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), repeat customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, profitability analysis, and geographical insights across countries, states, and cities. Interactive dashboards built with Streamlit and Plotly provide an intuitive way to explore key business metrics and identify growth opportunities.

🚀 Key Features
Customer Analysis & Segmentation
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis
Repeat vs Single-Time Customer Analysis
Sales & Profit Performance Tracking
Product and Category Analysis
Regional & Geographical Sales Insights
Monthly & Yearly Sales Trends
Interactive Streamlit Dashboard
Data Visualization with Plotly
Business KPI Monitoring

📈 Business Impact

This dashboard enables organizations to identify high-value customers, optimize marketing strategies, improve product performance, monitor sales growth, and make informed business decisions through data-driven insights.
